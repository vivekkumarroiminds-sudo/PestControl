Ant Control

Stop the trail at its source

Ants are persistent. The few you see on the counter are scouts from a much larger colony, and spraying them only removes the visible workers while the colony keeps producing more. Real ant control means reaching the nest.

We identify the species and the trails, then use methods that carry treatment back to the colony so the whole nest is eliminated — not just the ants on the surface.

Signs of an ant problem

  • Steady trails of ants along counters, walls or floors
  • Activity around the kitchen, sinks, windows or pet food
  • Small piles of soil or debris near foundations, walkways or driveways
  • Ants reappearing within days of being wiped away
  • Winged ants indoors, which can signal a nearby established colony

How Our Ant Control Works

  • We identify the ant species and follow the trails to locate nesting areas
  • We treat at the source so the colony — not just the visible ants — is eliminated
  • We protect entry points and perimeters to block new trails
  • We recommend simple steps to keep food and moisture from attracting them back

Every treatment is carried out by licensed, trained technicians and backed by our 100% satisfaction guarantee. If pests return between scheduled visits, so do we.
