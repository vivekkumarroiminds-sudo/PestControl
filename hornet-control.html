Hornet Control

Aggressive stingers, handled by professionals

Hornets are among the most intimidating stinging insects around the home. Their nests can grow large, and they defend them aggressively — hornet stings are more painful than typical wasp stings and can be dangerous, particularly for anyone with an allergy.

Removing a hornet nest is not a DIY job. Our technicians remove nests safely with the right protective equipment and methods, then help keep them from returning.

Signs of a hornet problem

  • A large papery nest in a tree, shrub, eave or wall void
  • Hornets repeatedly entering and exiting a single opening
  • Aggressive defensive behavior when you approach an area
  • Loud buzzing concentrated around one part of the property
  • Increased stinging-insect activity near doorways, decks or play areas

How Our Hornet Control Works

  • We carefully locate and assess the nest and confirm the species
  • We remove the nest safely using full protective methods
  • We treat the area to handle returning hornets and reduce attractants
  • We advise on monitoring so a new nest does not go unnoticed

Every treatment is carried out by licensed, trained technicians and backed by our 100% satisfaction guarantee. If pests return between scheduled visits, so do we.
