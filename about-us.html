About The Company

A more thoughtful approach to home pest control

Pest Control Emergency was built around a simple idea: pest control should feel clear and reassuring, not stressful. We show up with a plan, explain what matters, and treat your home with the care good service should have.

We focus on residential homes and the pest problems we are best equipped to handle well — from ants, roaches and spiders to rodents and stinging insects. Every visit follows the same thoughtful process, from inspection through follow-up, so nothing is left to chance.

Our technicians are licensed and trained, our treatments are chosen with your family in mind, and our work is backed by a 100% satisfaction guarantee.
