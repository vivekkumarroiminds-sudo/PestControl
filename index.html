Our Home Treatment Approach

A more thoughtful way to treat a home

Every visit follows a clear approach built around where pests live, how they get in, and what helps keep them from coming back.

1. Inspect

Start with a careful inspection of the pest, the home, and the visible signs of activity.

2. Locate

Look closely at entry points, edges, gaps, moisture, and hidden problem zones.

3. Target

Treat the areas that actually matter, not just the areas that are easiest to reach.

4. Protect

Build strong protective coverage around the home to help reduce future pest activity.

5. Follow Up

Keep the next step clear when the situation needs continued attention.
