Wasp Control

Handle wasps safely — leave the nest to the pros

Wasps become a real problem when they nest close to where your family lives and plays. Disturbing a nest can provoke aggressive, repeated stings, which is dangerous for anyone — and especially for those with allergies. This is one pest where DIY is rarely worth the risk.

Licensed local technicians remove nests and treat stinging-insect activity safely, then help reduce the conditions that attract wasps back to your property.

Signs of a wasp problem

  • A nest under eaves, in the garage, shed, or wall voids
  • A steady stream of wasps entering and leaving the same spot
  • Increased activity around trash cans, food or sweet drinks outdoors
  • Wasps repeatedly gathering around a deck, patio or doorway
  • Aggressive behavior when you are near a particular area of the yard

How a Typical Wasp Control Program Works

  • Local providers locate the nest and identify the stinging insect involved
  • They treat and remove the nest safely, using proper protective methods
  • They address attractants and entry points to discourage rebuilding
  • They advise on keeping the area wasp-free through the season

Every treatment is carried out by licensed local technicians, and many providers back their work with a satisfaction guarantee — if covered pests return between scheduled visits, so do they.
